Recent activities?

PROJECTS
MHD Free Convection with Joule Heating and Entropy Generation inside an H-shaped Chamber

Year: 2023

The research investigates the natural convective flow of water in an H-shaped chamber subjected to an external magnetic field and Joule heating. Numerical simulations are performed for different chamber configurations, varying parameters like Rayleigh and Hartmann numbers. Heat transfer performance is evaluated using Nusselt numbers, fluid temperature, entropy generation, and thermal criteria. Streamlines and isotherms illustrate fluid flow and heat transfer patterns.

Research Partner: Riyan Hashem Jamy (ME, BUET)

Air Conditioner Using Peltier Module

Year: 2023

This project aims to create an air conditioner using a thermoelectric module (Peltier) and readily available local components. The design process covers Peltier module selection, fin and fan motor selection, electrical circuit design, and pump selection. SolidWorks was initially used for the design, and the Peltier-Thermoelectric Cooler Module Calculator powered by TE Technology was employed for Peltier module selection. Additionally, an economic analysis was conducted to consider the manufacturing of the air conditioner.

Project Partners: Shorup Chanda (ME, BUET) & Riyan Hashem Jamy (ME, BUET)

Unmanned Aerial Vehicle (UAV)

Year: 2022

This UAV (Unmanned Aerial Vehicle) is designed primarily for automated package delivery to fixed locations. It utilizes GPS (Global Positioning System) for precise navigation and delivery. The key benefits of this UAV include efficient and rapid delivery, cost reduction, and the ability to deliver packages to challenging and remote locations.

Project Partners: Sajib Ali (ME, BUET), Parvez Hossen (ME, BUET) & Sanowar Hossain (ME, BUET)
